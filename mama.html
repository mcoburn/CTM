Mama’s Holiday Wish List Meme

TodaysMama (link to: http://bit.ly/tmwishlist) and GameStop (link to: http://bit.ly/gamestop10) are giving away a sleighful of gifts this holiday season and to enter I’m sharing this meme with you.

1.    What is your holiday wish for your family?

a.    That everyone is healthy and happy in the new year and that the business that we are trying to start takes  off. That those in the military are kept safe and continue to serve their country, and spend time with their family as much as possible.

2.    What is your Christmas morning tradition?

a.    Every Winter Solstice we all get  bundled up and go to the beach to greet the sun as it rises and think about a new year. Then we drive home, and de-bundle back  into our PJs and open the presents that Santa brought during the night. We have a big family Dinner, and extended family sometimes comes to help us celebrate. Family is the most important thing.

3.    If you could ask Santa for one, completely decadent wish for yourself, what would it be?

a.    A cruise vacation anywhere (for the whole family), with some real pampering on the way and exotic cultural experiences when we got there! That would be awesome! Or if it could be anything, the Beautiful live-aboard boat that we could retire on and a significant chunk of change to start with.

4.    How do you make the holidays special without spending any money?

a.    Spend them with family and do things together, away from distractions and things like the television. Quality AND quantity. A nice drive through the neighborhood to see all of the lights.

5.    What games did you play with your family growing up?

a.    Anything with a board. Monopoly was a big hit, as were Sorry, Trouble, Trivial Pursuit, Scrabble, Uno, Candy Land, Risk and Cranium.

6.    What holiday tradition have you carried on from your own childhood?

a.    Opening a single present on the night before. Usually new PJs, which you then sleep in and take pictures of, the next morning.

7.    Where would you go for a Christmas-away-from-home trip?

a.    To the Grandparents!

a.    The XBOX 360
